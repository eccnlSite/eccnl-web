Outreach

So many things to do. Life can be so confusing. So many things to worry about. And sometimes, so many fears and so many hurts.

We believe in Jesus Christ and the promise of forgiveness of sin through him.

Our goal is to grow and mature together as a group to be more like Christ, to meet the spiritual needs of our community, and to support one another.
