About Our Church

Emmanuel Chinese Church is a Chinese Christian Church in St. John’s, Newfoundland and Labrador started in January, 2012.

 

It was founded by a vision based on the Bible to include all Chinese of different mother tongues, Revelation 7: 9 and 10. We use English (main language), Mandarin and Cantonese in our Worship Services and Bible Studies. Though most of us have a Chinese cultural background all are welcome and encouraged to come and see what our Lord Jesus Christ has in store for all of us, Matthew 6: 20.
